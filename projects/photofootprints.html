Photograph & Footprints

Background

Photographs & Footprints is a 3D, first-person simulator made in Unity, aimed to teach players about wildlife exploration, the animals habitating it, and how to take care of the environment through photography. The game seeks to provide a calming atmosphere, where players can relax, while also learning how to respect the wildlife and tidbits of photography. As a team of five, we spent roughly 4 months developing this game for our capstone project, hoping to provide a peaceful space for those who needed or wanted to slow down.

As the game writer and sound designer, I developed and designed the dialogue scenes and its UI, developed the skeleton of the shop UI, implemented basic sounds for immersion, and collaborated with my teammates to edit and rescope certain sections to hit our goals without dipping into areas outside of the project's scope.
